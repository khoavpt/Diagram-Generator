What is Diagram Generator?

Diagram Generator is a tool to help you create diagrams for your geometry Olympiad problems. Simply type in the description of your problems and wait for the result. It can be used to create simple geometric figures, like points and lines, or more complicated figures, like circles and polygons, with lots of different customizations.


How it works?

The process can be divided into two different parts: Extract information and generate diagrams.

Information from the user’s inputs is extracted using a technique called rule-based matching, which is extracting information in a document using a combination of patterns. In this program, I only use some simple patterns that often appear in Vietnamese geometry Olympiad problems, which answers why the program still struggles a lot in trying to understand long and complex sentences.

And finally, the diagram generation part was done using GMB(Geometry Model Builder). Extracted information are converted to GMBL(Geometry Model Builder Language), which is required for GMB to generate diagrams.

An example

Bài toán (Thầy Nguyễn Minh Hà): Cho tam giác ABC, (O), H theo thứ tự là đường tròn ngoại tiếp và trực tâm. T nằm trên đường tròn (O). E, F theo thứ tự là giao điểm của AB, AC và đường thẳng qua H song song với AT. I là tâm ngoại tiếp đường tròn (AEF). Chứng minh H, I, T thẳng hàng

Supported operations

Here is the list of all operations supported in the Diagram Generator:

When introducing a new geometric object:
- Introduce a new object unrelated to other objects (Cho tam giác ABC, vẽ tứ giác ABCD, cho đường tròn delta, kẻ đường thẳng d, ...)
- Introduce a point on a line, a ray or a segment (Điểm A nằm trên đường thẳng delta, A thuộc đoạn thẳng BC, điểm A nằm ở tia BC, ...)
- Introduce a point on a circle (D thuộc đường tròn delta, ...)
- Introduce a point as the circumcenter, centroid, or orthocenter of a triangle (Trực tâm của tam giác ABC là H, G là trọng tâm của tam giác ABC, ...)
- Introduce a point as the intersection of two lines (AB cắt CD tại M, đường thẳng delta cắt đoạn BC tại K, ...)
- Introduce a point inside a polygon (điểm M nằm trong tứ giác ABCD, ...)
- Introduce a point as the midpoint of a segment (M là trung điểm đoạn BC, ...)
- Introduce a line or a circle passing through a point (đường thẳng delta đi qua A, đường tròn beta đi qua B, ...)
- Introduce a line tangent to a circle (đường thẳng delta tiếp xúc đường tròn beta, ...)
- Introduce a line as the perpendicular bisector of a segment (đường thẳng delta là trung trực đoạn AB, ...)
- Introduce a circle tangent to another circle (đường tròn delta tiếp xúc với đường tròn beta, ...)
- Introduce a circle centered at a point (đường tròn delta có tâm O, ...)
- Introduce a circle as the circumcircle or incircle of a triangle

When asserting conditions between different geometry objects:
- Assert two triangles to be congruent with each other. (tam giác ABC đồng dạng với tam giác MNP, ...)
- Assert two lines to be parallel or perpendicular to each other (Đường thẳng delta vuông góc với BC, AB song song MN, ...)
- Assert two segments to have the same length.
- Assert a point to be the midpoint of a segment.
- Assert a point to be the centroid, circumcenter, or orthocenter of a triangle
- Assert a point to be on a line, segment, ray, or a circle

Tips for better results

Here are some tips and tricks to get the most out of Diagram Generator:
1. Use short and simple sentences!
DG extract information using very simple patterns so it won't be to handle long and complex sentences. Don’t copy the whole problem, instead, try to rephrase it into instructions that the program can follow.
2. Introduce only one geometry object per sentence.
3. Don’t make spelling mistakes.
DG doesn’t have auto-correction so it’s best not to misspell any words!
4. Try to specify the type (point, line, circle, ...) when you mention a geometry object.
5. And finally, be patient! Large and complex diagrams might take a few minutes to generate.
